About Us

We are Sip of Ann Arbor, a group of students from the University of Michigan whose goal is to create a recipe book for current students and Umich alumni to create their favorite cocktails from the best Ann Arbor bars and restaurants! Our recipe book is available in the "store" tab, and with each purchase, you receive a one-time code to be used for virtual access to our recipes!
