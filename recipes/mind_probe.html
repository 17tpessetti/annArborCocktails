Rick's

Mind Probe

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Gin
  • 2 oz Rum
  • 2 oz Tequila
  • 3 oz Pineapple Juice
  • 2 oz Triple Sec
  • 3 oz Sprite
  • 1 splash Grenadine
  • 1 squeeze Lemon Juice

Directions

  1. Pour all ingredients into a shaker and shake
  2. Pour into a glass over ice and serve
