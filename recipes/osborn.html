The Last Word

The Osborn

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of Pineapple
  • 1 -3 slices of Jalepeño
  • 2 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Lime Juice
  • 1/4 oz Simple Syrup
  • 1 oz Ginger Beer
  • 1 slice of Cucumber
    (optional garnish)

Directions

  1. Muddle pineapple and jalepeño in a shaker
  2. Add gin, lime, and simple syrup to the shaker and shake
  3. Double strain into a rocks glass and top with ginger beer
  4. Optional: Garnish with a cucumber slice
