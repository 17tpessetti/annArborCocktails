Electric Kool-Aid

Ingredients

  • 1/2 oz Amaretto
  • 1/2 oz Blue Curacao
  • 1/2 oz Midori
  • 1/2 oz Peachtree Schnapps
  • 2 dashes Angostura Bitters
  • 1/2 can Sprite

Directions

  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Add amaretto, blue curacao, midori, and schnapps to a shaker with ice and shake
  3. Strain into the glass
  4. Add bitters and top off with sprite
