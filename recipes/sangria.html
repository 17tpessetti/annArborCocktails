Sangria

Ingredients

  • 4 cups Cranberry Juice
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 4 Citrus Fruits - quartered
    (lemons, limes, and organges)
  • 1/2 cup Brandy
  • 1 bottle Manischewitz Wine
    (Concord Grape Flavor)
  • 3 bottles Red Table Wine

Directions

  1. Mix the juice, sugar, and fruit in a large pot. Bring to a boil, stirring often.
  2. Lower the heat and simmer for 45 minutes. Let the mixture cool.
  3. Once the mixture has cooled, pour in all remaining ingredients and stir.
  4. Serve over ice in glasses or mason jars.
