Mint Julep

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 oz Kentucky Spirit Bourbon
  • 5 mint leaves
  • 1/2 oz Simple Syrup

Directions

  1. Place mint leaves and simple syrup into a glass and muddle
  2. Add bourbon on top of the muddled mixture
  3. Fill the same glass with ice and stir the contents
