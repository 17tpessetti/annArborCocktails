Frita Batidos

Boozy Batido

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup Crushed Ice
  • 2.5 scoops Guernsey Vanilla Ice Cream
  • 1/3 cup Monin Lime Syrup
  • 1 tbsp Lime Juice
  • 1/2 oz White Rum

Directions

  1. Place ice, ice cream, syrup, lime juice, and rum in a blender.
  2. Blend ingredients until smooth and serve in a cold glass.
