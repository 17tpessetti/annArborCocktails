The Raven's Club

High 75

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Toki Japanese whiskey
  • 1/2 oz Honey (or simple syrup)
  • 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
  • Sparkling Wine of your choice

Directions

  1. Add the whiskey, lemon juice, and syrup to a shaker with ice
  2. Shake until chilled
  3. Strain into a champagne flute
  4. Top off with sparkling wine
