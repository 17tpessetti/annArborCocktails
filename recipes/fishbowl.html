Good Time Charley's

Original Fishbowl

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 oz Raspberry Vodka
  • 1 1/2 oz Island Blue Pucker
  • 1/3 cup Lemonade
  • 1/2 can Sprite

Directions

  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Pour all drink ingredients into the glass over ice
  3. Optional: Garnish with a lemon wedge
