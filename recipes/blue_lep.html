Blue Leprechaun

The Blue Leprechaun

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Smirnoff Sour Lemon Berry Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Citrus Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Blueberry Vodka
  • 0.5 oz Blue Curacao
  • 2/3 can Sprite

Directions

  1. Fill a glass with ice
  2. Pour smirnoff sour, citrus, and blueberry vodka, and blue curacao over the ice
  3. Top with sprite and stir
