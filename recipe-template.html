The Last Word

You're My Boy Blue

Ingredients

  • 8 Blueberries
  • 1 3/4 oz Bourbon
  • 3/4 oz Elderflower Liquor
  • 1/2 oz Lemon Juice
  • 1 bsp Simple Syrup
  • 1 Lemon Peel Twist
    (optional garnish)

Directions

  1. Muddle blueberries in a glass
  2. Combine bourbon, elderflower liquor, lemon juice, and simple syrup
  3. Shake and double strain the liquid combination
  4. Pour into a glass over blueberries and garnish with a lemon peel twist
